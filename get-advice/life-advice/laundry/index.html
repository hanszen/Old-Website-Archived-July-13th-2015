    Laundry

    We're working on getting you a photo (or perhaps video) guide on how to work the laundry machines at Hanszen/Rice, but for now, just remember...

    • Separate lights and darks. (You don't want pink clothes now, do you?)
    • Don't use too much detergent (& use High Efficiency aka HE)
    • DON'T PUT SOMEONE'S CLOTHES IN THE DRYER FOR THEM. It may seem like a nice gesture at the time, but some people have shrinkable clothes & some items of clothing never go in the dryer. (like bras)
    • Treat other's clothes how you would want yours to be treated.

    Also you could check out this e-how article.

    Want to know when your clothes will be done (you can even set up text or e-mail alerts!), when peak laundry use times are, or just when the next laundry machine will be open? Look no further than Rice's laundry link website.

    Problems with the laundry room? E-mail the SPAF committee heads! Although if a machine is broken, you should e-mail work@rice.edu instead.
