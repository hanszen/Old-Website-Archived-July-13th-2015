Presidential Update

Hey Hanszen!

We have come once again to the most wonderful time of the year: Willy Week. Like always, we're going to make every other student on campus wish they'd been placed in Hanszen. That's just how we roll. So, here's the plan:

1) Wreck shop with jacks. Last night, we hit all 9 other colleges. Tonight, we're gonna show Will Rice that no unjackables list is insurmountable. Tomorrow, we burn and pillage. Or something like that.

2) FILL ALL THE BALLOONS! As of yesterday, Martel had six trash cans full of balloons before we finished our first. Unacceptable. We DOMINATED the water balloon fight last year, which means that according to Viking custom everyone should be paying us tribute so we don't lay waste to them again. No tribute=no mercy. FILL FILL FILL

3) Watch Rob rep our college in the Beer Debates!!! A bunch of lame RAs have decided they can take on our master, the man with the intelligence of a hundred professors and the alcohol tolerance of a thousand UT frat boys. We're gonna fill up Willy's pub at 8:00 Tuesday night to make sure that Rob's the only one with a cheering section. But seriously, this might actually be the best thing ever to happen in the history of life.

4) There's this race on Saturday that we're gonna win fo sho. So be there. Over/under on the number of bikers Matthew Charles laps = 12.

In other news:

A) Last Wednesday at lunch, two members of the Rice Board of Trustees came to eat lunch at Hanszen. They were the lucky ones. Evidently the trustees drew straws to see who would get to eat at Hanszen. Seriously. The New Student Section Advisory Board got to present to them about why we love Hanszen as well as our hopes and dreams for a possible new New Section. We also had a great conversation about the challenges of rising enrollment and the role of the college system in campus life.

B) Right now, the campus is voting on an increase in the Blanket Tax for Beer Bike. Because of rising costs, without more money Beer Bike might not happen next year. So go vote!

C) For those of you wondering what to do with yourselves after Beer Bike, have no fear! The Hanszen Talent Show (April 13th), the Inaugural Hanszen Olympics (April 21st), Associates Dinner/Awards Night, and more are still to come!

If you guys have any concerns, big ideas, or just want to chat, then please find me in the commons. But seriously.

HFH!
Henry
