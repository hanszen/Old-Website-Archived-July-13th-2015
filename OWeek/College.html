About the College System

The college system at Rice is probably the closest you will ever get to going to Hogwarts, albeit with eleven colleges instead of four houses. These colleges are: Hanszen (the best one!), Baker, Will Rice, Wiess, Jones, Brown, Lovett, Sid Rich, Martel, McMurtry, and Duncan. Students in each college have a wide range of majors, interests, and activities, so you're sure to find someone else who shares your passions or who can offer advice on practically anything. An important aspect of each college is the number of people who are here to support you in a number of ways. These include the Masters, the RAs, the College Coordinator, O-week coordinators, academic advisors, and a host of others (don't worry – you'll get to meet them all during O-week).

Each college has their own form of student government, which includes the President, Justices, and class representatives. Our student government is called Cabinet, and you'll be able to find links to more information about that above. There are also many committees in charge of overseeing various aspects of college life, whether it be socials, ambiance, or sports. Getting involved with your college is a great way to meet new people and help make your home away from home an even more awesome place to live.
