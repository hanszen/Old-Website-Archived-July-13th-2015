Hanszen College Theatre is operated by students at Rice University. Anyone is welcome to come audition or help. Students from all of the colleges audition for roles, and students and alumni from across campus show up to help with technical and artistic aspects of the production. The group's administrative body, the Musical Committee, is made up entirely of student volunteers from Hanszen College (the best residential college at Rice!). These students are responsible for planning the upcoming musical each spring. This year's Musical Committee co-chairs are Morgan Robinson and Gwen Thomas.


Hanszen College Theatre is amateur musical theatre produced on the Rice University campus with the support of Hanszen College.


All of our productions take place at Rice University in the Hanszen College Commons (dining hall).


Productions typically open on the second weekend in November with auditions in the early weeks of the fall semester. This gives new freshmen the opportunity to audition and allows for almost two months of rehearsal time prior to opening.


I don't know who started Hanszen College Theater or why they did. We continue the tradition because we want to see quality musical performed on the Rice campus.


We are only able to produce these wonderful shows with the support of our volunteers, the men and women of Hanszen College, the Hanszen College Masters, and our audiences. Thank you.
