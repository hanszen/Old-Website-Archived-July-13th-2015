Cy Coleman's pulsating and intricate score mirrors and adds to the live action. The musical style is predominately jazz and bebop. David Zippel's witty lyrics are full of double meanings that add to the already intricate show.

The music is performed by a live orchestra under the direction of musical director Dave Gibbons.
