HANSZEN HISTORY HISTORY HISTORY HISTORY HISTORY

It has been said that colleges at Rice have a quick turnover, and that we quickly forget the past. This may be true, but not for all of us. Things have changed drastically from Hanszen's birth just over 40 years ago, but several things remain the same. First, dedicated Hanszenites have devoted much of their stay at Rice to bettering their college, for themselves, for their friends, and for the future. Secondly, inspired Hanszenites have made important contributions to this University- From The Corner for the Dreaming Monkey and KHCR to Rice Cakes - with the help of other dedicated Hanszenites. And finally, we still hate Wiess.

During the past year, we have created a mini- historical section in our upper loft library. These pictures come from 4 photo albums we keep, along side copies of past Hanszen documents - The Tower, The Liason, O-Week Books, Ross Goldberg's History Of Hanszen College, etc. The documents located here, as well as others to big to have yet translated them to the web, are there as well.

The pictures here represent my best guess as to time period - no doubt I have missed the marks in some. If you recognize a mistake, please let me know.

Finally, if you're ever in town, and interested in stopping bye to share some old memories, please give us a call.
